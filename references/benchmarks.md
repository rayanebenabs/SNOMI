### Creator tier multipliers

*Applied to the base CPM to estimate the actual cost per 1,000 impressions by tier.*

| Tier     | Follower range | CPM multiplier | Rationale |
|----------|----------------|----------------|-----------|
| Nano     | 1k–10k         | × 0.5–0.7      | Highly engaged audiences but limited reach; negotiated cost/impression below market rate |
| Micro    | 10k–100k       | × 0.8–1.0      | Optimal cost/engagement profile; reference close to base |
| Mid-tier | 100k–500k      | × 1.0 *(base)* | SNOMI standard reference |
| Macro    | 500k–1M        | × 1.3–1.7      | Premium for guaranteed reach and creator brand equity |
| Mega     | > 1M           | × 1.8–3.0      | Celebrity premium, exclusivity, and perceived brand safety |

> **Nano paradox:** negotiated CPM is lower, but CPE (VAL.002) is often stronger. Never compare VAL.003 across tiers without adjusting for campaign objective (reach vs. engagement).

### Production complexity multipliers

*Captures the value of creative work independently of reach.*

| Level | Label             | Definition                                                                                                                                                          | Multiplier |
|-------|-------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------|
| C1    | Light             | Phone-shot, native platform format, solo creator, short production time (Stories, native TikTok, selfie-style)                                                      | × 0.6–0.8  |
| C2    | Standard *(base)* | Planned shoot, curated editing, solo or small team — the majority of Reels and curated feed posts                                                                   | × 1.0      |
| C3    | Heavy             | Professional film crew, long artisanal creation (fashion-making, ceramics, elaborate recipe), significant post-production, or long-form YouTube with scripting and full edit | × 1.8–3.0 |

> **Application example (FR, Instagram):**
> Nano creator (×0.6) · artisanal Heavy content (×2.2) → estimated CPM = €4.80 × 0.6 × 2.2 = **≈ €6.30**
> This CPM exceeds the base despite the small tier: production complexity justifies the premium.

> **Validation rule:** if the negotiated CPM exceeds ×3.5 the base reference without tier or complexity justification, request a creator explanation or verify the calculation method.

## CRE.003 — Collaboration Rate Risk Thresholds (all markets)

| Rate     | Signal           | Interpretation |
|----------|------------------|----------------|
| < 20%    | Healthy          | Low saturation risk, audience trusts organic content |
| 20–40%   | Monitor          | Watch for engagement drop on sponsored posts |
| > 40%    | Saturation Risk  | Audience may discount sponsored content; flag in BFS |
